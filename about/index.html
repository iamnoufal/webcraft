About the
Robin Hood Club

Robin Hood Club, a dynamic community of tech enthusiasts and innovators who share a passion for using their skills to make the world a better place. Our club is dedicated to organizing hackathons, workshops, and competitions that bring together like-minded individuals to collaborate, learn, and create cutting-edge solutions to real-world problems.

At the Robin Hood Club, we believe that technology can be a force for good, and we strive to use our skills to create positive change in our communities and beyond. Whether you're an experienced programmer or just starting out, we welcome everyone who is passionate about using technology to make a difference.

02. Debugging Mania

DebuggingMania - A Treasure Hunt Competition where you, along with your teammates can unwind the solutions using clues and Hints provided. If you got what it takes to reach the treasure, here is a paradise for your hunting and fun.

DebuggingMania was conducted in a Two Rounds format where the first round was a Quiz Round taken on a Quiz platform in a proctored mode. The 20 minutes Quiz was held on 14th August 2022 from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM IST consisting of 25 logical clue-based problems.

And the second round was a riddle-based answering round taken for the selected candidates from the first round, on a Google form on the same day from 6:30 PM to 7:30 PM IST.
