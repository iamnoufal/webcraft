Rules and Regulations

  • Eligibility:The hackathon is open to all BS Degree students who have a passion for designing and web development.
  • Team Size:Participants have to compete individually.
  • Registration:Interested participants must register for the hackathon by filling out the registration form ( With your Official IITM Student Email ID) provided by the organizers and Paradox Team from the official website of Paradox.
  • Themes: Participants are free to choose any theme for their website wireframe design, based on the domains provided by the organizers.
  • Wireframing Round: Participants must design and submit their wireframes within the allotted time during the first round.
  • Plagiarism/Cheating: No plagiarism or using plugin/AI plugin used for making the wireframe or coding the Website will be tolerated. Expert can understand the work of AI or plugins
  • Code of Conduct: Participants must adhere to the hackathon's code of conduct, which includes respecting others, maintaining a positive attitude, and not engaging in any form of cheating.
  • Intellectual Property: All code, designs, and other materials created during the hackathon will be the intellectual property of the participants and we as organizers will reserve the right to showcase and forward your work to the judges and the user-experience team only for the purpose of evaluation.
  • Disqualification: Participants who violate the rules and regulations or the hackathon's code of conduct will be disqualified from the competition.
  • Organizers' Discretion:The organizers reserve the right to make changes to the rules and regulations, disqualify participants, or cancel the event at their discretion.
  • Liability: The organizers will not be held responsible for any loss, injury, or damage incurred by participants during the event. Participants are responsible for their own safety and well-being.

The Interview and other hiring activities details and their rules and regulations will be shared at the later stages and the participants are expected to have fun in the hackathon along with maintaining a level of professionalism among all stages and even in the interview stages. Defaulters not following the code of conduct at any level will result in disqualification, even if it is the interview or hiring process stage.
